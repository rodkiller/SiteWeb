- Regularly publish the information, regulations, procedure manuals and administrative documents whose access is free.

These provisions strengthen administrative transparency and open governance.

    • Registration of a birth
        Documents required :
        Give the information required either by:
      • declaration by the one who has noted the birth.
      • declaration of the hospital or clinic where the birth took place.
      • Anyone who has attended birth.

        Present any document establishing the identity of the father or mother of the newborn, tel.than :

      • The national identity card of the father or mother.
      • or the family booklet.
      • or the birth certificate of one of the sons.

        Signature of the declarant in the birth register.

        observation :

        The purpose of the presentation of the documents is to avoid errors and to ensure the conformity of the writing of the names of the members of the same family.
        Stages of the service: - The person concerned must:

      • apply to the civil status officer.
      • Provide the required information.
      • Inform from the place of birth.
      • Sign the 2 copies of the birth register.

        Stakeholders :
        Civil status officers of The municipal borough

        Place of deposit of the file:
        Civil status office of The municipal borough

        Place of obtaining the benefit:
        Civil status office of The municipal borough

        NB: Registration must take place within 10 days of birth.
        Beyond The Legal Deadline, Registration Can Only Be Done On Authorization
        Tribunal.

        LEGISLATIVE AND OR REGULATORY REFERENCES:

      • Articles 22,23,24,25 and 27 of Law No 57-3 of 1 August 1957 regulating the civil status.
      • Articles 3.7 and 10 of Law No. 65-31DU 24/7/1965 on the civil status.
      • Circular of the first ministry n ° 15 of 14 February 1989.
    • Conclusion of a marriage contract
        Conditions of obtaining:
        Future husbands must fulfill all legal conditions of marriage:
      • Have reached legal age and failing to leave the court.
      • The consent of future husbands.
      • A confirmation made by two honorable witnesses of the exemption of the future spouses of all cases of prevention prescribed by law.
      • The fixing of a dowry for the benefit of the wife.

        Documents required :
      • Birth certificate of each of the two spouses
      • Prenuptial medical certificate.
      • Photocopy of the national identity card or other official document justifying the identity of each of the two spouses.
      • Authorization of the Tribunal for those who have not reached the legal age.
      • Written agreement by the guardian by authentic act if he does not attend the conclusion of the marriage, and that for the man and for the woman who did not reach the majority.
      • Extract from the death certificate of the husband or woman for widowers and widows, shipping the divorce judgment for divorces.
      • Administrative authorization for those who are internal security forces, subject to prior marriage authorization such as active agents, customs and national army.
      • Certification of the consulate or the embassy attesting to the possibility of contracting the marriage for foreigners.
      • Certificate of conversion to Islam for non-Muslims who wish to marry with a Muslim Tunisian.
      • Presentation of the identity card for each of the two witnesses who must meet the required legal conditions.

        Stages of the service:
        Interested (both spouses or their representative with aOfficial power of attorney) must:
      • The assistance to constitute files that they present to the competent services of the Ministry of Social Affairs and Solidarity.
      • Apply to the civil status officer.
      • Provide the required parts.
      • Set a date for the conclusion of marriage.
      • The interested parties and the witnesses and the civil status officer must sign the marriage contract

        Speakers:
        Civil status officers of The municipal borough

        Place of Deposit of the File:
        civil status office of The municipal borough
        Place of Obtaining The Benefit:
        Civil Status Office of The Municipal Arrondissement

        Time to obtain the service:
        Of the order of two days to a week

        Legislative and or regulatory references:
      • Articles 15 and of 32 at 39 of the law n° 57 from 1er August 1957 regulating the civil status.
      • Decree 13 August 1956 promulgating the Code of Personal Status and the texts that have changed it.
      • The particular statutes relating to the active bodies of the internal security forces, agents of customs, military forces and diplomatic agents.
    • Death inscription
        Conditions of obtaining: in case of death.

        Documents required :
      • Provide the maximum information on the deceased and present if it is possible its birth certificate or national identity card.
      • Report of security services in the event of death in dubious circumstances.

        Stages of the service:
        The registrant must:
      • Apply to the registrar
      • Provide the required information (In case the death took place at the hospital: sign the 2 copies of the declarant death register).

        Observations :
      • The declarant close to the deceased or anyone with accurate and complete information as much as possible.
      • Beyond the legal deadline (3 days ), Registration can only be done on the authorization of the Tribunal.
      • Be satisfied with the statement made by the hospital to avoid dual death.

        Stakeholders :
        Civil status officers of The municipal borough

        Filing location of the file :
      • Civil status office of The municipal borough
      • The conservation of Muslim cemeteries

        Lieu d'obtention de la prestation :
      • Civil status office of the municipal district.
      • The conservation of Muslim cemeteries

        Time to obtain the service :
        Immediately or within the deadlines set by the law regulating the civil status (Ajuras).

        Legislative and regulatory references:
        43 In the article 58 N ° 57-3 From August 1, 1957 regulating the marital status and the texts that have modified it.
    • Birth extract / wedding extract / death extract
        Obtaining conditions: Just formulate a request.
        Parts to provide:
      • Act information (number, year, day) .
      • Other civil status document of the person concerned indicating whether it is an original act or a declaration or judgment (for birth extract) .
      • For the marriage certificate: specify whether the act has been concluded at the municipality or with two notaries.
      • payment of royalties due (cash payment or postal mandate) on behalf of the recipe manager With the municipality with a stamped envelope bearing the applicant's address, if the request is made by post.

        Stages of the service:
        The registrant must:
      • Apply to the registry officer.
      • Provide the necessary information: for the birth extract (present a civil status document specifying the number, the year and The day of the act mentioning if it is an original act or a declaration or judgment) and for the wedding extract (specify if the act has been concluded at the municipality or with two notaries) .

        Observations:
      • Civil status extracts are issued to the holder of the act, his ascendants, his descendants, his guardian, his legal representative, his spouse if he is neither separated nor divorced .
      • Administrative extracts are issued free of charge to the prosecutor of the Republic and other administrative services.
      • Resident Citizens Office Abroad (birth extract).
      • The preservation of the Muslim cemeteries (death extract) .

        Speakers:
        Civil status officers of The municipal district

        place of deposit of the file:
      • Vital status office of The arrondissement MUNICIPAL
      • Central service for those born before 1965 (birth extract, wedding extract, extract from death).

        Place of obtaining the service:
      • Vital status service of The arrondissement MUNICIPAL
      • Central service for those born before 1965 (birth extract, wedding extract, death extract).
      • Resident Citizens Office Abroad (birth extract).
      • The preservation of the Muslim cemeteries (death extract) .

        Time to obtain the service:
        Immediately or after 24h after the date of filing the application.

        Legislative and regulatory references:
      • Law N ° 57-3 of 1 August 1957 regulating the civil status.
      • Law N ° 64-42 of the 24 November 1964 of civil status.
      • Prime Minister's Circular No. 15 February 14, 1989 .
    • Attribution d'un nom de famille aux personnes qui ne l'ont pas sur leur acte de naissance
        Conditions of obtaining:
      • The person concerned must have a family name different from that of other members of his family.
      • it does not have to use the new name assigned in its official documents (identity card) .

        Parts to provide:
      • requests on behalf of the Minister of the Interior and local development bearing the full address of the person concerned with his legalized signature.
      • Recent birth extract of the interested party mentioning the number and date of the decree under which it has been assigned a new name (Different from that of the other brothers) .
      • birth extract (or death) of the father of the person concerned.
      • recent birth extracts of the brothers of the person concerned with different names to his, or declaration on the honor of the person concerned in which he quotes all the first names of his live brothers , or specifies that it has absolutely no lifetime brothers or it is not possible to present an excerpt from his father's death.
      • Photocopy of the national identity card of the person concerned.

        Stages of the service:
      • Constitute a complete folder and send it to the Civil Status Office of the Borough.
      • Study the folder and prepare a correct text.
      • Transmit the text to the first department for approval and publication in the Official Journal of the Republic of Tunisia.
      • Transmit the draft text after its publication to the municipality concerned to mention the rectification of the birth certificate of the person concerned.

        Speakers:
        The civil status officers of The municipal borough

        Place of deposit of the file:
        Civil status office of Municipal borough

        Place of obtaining the service:
        The municipal district

        Time to obtain the service:
        two months.

        Legislative and or regulatory references:
        N ° 59-53 of the May 26, 1959 making the acquisition by each Tunisian mandatory a patronymic name.
    • Inhumation order
        Conditions of obtaining:
        The inhumation order can only be issued after death and before burial.

        Documents required :
      • A medical certificate indicating that death is natural.
      • Authorization of the Prosecutor of the Republic in the event of death in questionable circumstances.
      • Report written security services in case of non-natural death.
      • Royalty due.

        observations :
      • If death occurred in doubtful circumstances or if it is due to violence or an accident or other non-natural circumstances, the burial order is only given on the basis of the report written by the servicesnational security.
      • The civil status officer of the place where the mortal remains will be transported must establish the order of burial on the basis of the accompanying parts (Medical certificate and death from death) without having to investigate more about the causes of death.

        Stages of the service :
      • Submit a request to the Civil Status Officer of the Borough.
      • Provide the required information and parts.
      • Issuance of the inhumation order.

        Stakeholders :
        Civil status officers of The municipal borough

        Filing location of the file :
      • Civil status office of The municipal borough
      • The conservation of the cemeteries of Muslims.

        Place of obtaining the service :
      • Civil status office of The municipal borough
      • The conservation of the cemeteries of Muslims.

        Time to obtain the service :
        At once.

        Legislative and or regulatory references :
      • The articles 44, 45 and 48 of the civil status law n° 57 - 3 from 01/08/1957 and the texts that have modified it and completed.
      • The article 76 Organic law of municipalities n° 75 - 33 from 14/05/1975.
      • Ministry of the Interior and Local Development Circular n° 86 from 03/11/1997.

        Important recommendations :
        Anyone who is inhibiting a death without the order of burial is punishable by imprisonment and a fine.
